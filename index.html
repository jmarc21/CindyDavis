Over 90% of Utah parents enroll their children in district and charter schools. These combine to create public schools for kids. Cindy is uniquely qualified as both parent and practitioner to take a preK-post high school view of our education system. The Utah constitution calls for the Utah Board of Education to oversee public education in our state. Too often we have top-down unfunded mandates that reduce the nimbleness of schools and impair local ability to meet student needs. Cindy will work for policies that make sense for schools and families.
